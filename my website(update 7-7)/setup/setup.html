How I See Technology

I think it's important to get a brief talk about my mindest on technology here. I'm adopting the philosophy of digital minimalism, where you spend your online time the best and more productive way possible. As a start, i recommend you reading the self-titled book by Cal Newport.
You can read more about that at the lifestyle section.

You can buy the book on amazon
or you can download it here
(Highly recommend his other books.)

The philosophy includes a whole new way-of-thinking around the technology you use and the time you spend, wisely or not. If somewhy you struggle with screen time and this type of stuff, you gotta give Digital-Minimalism a try.
